Every year, the Government of Canada releases a detailed household consumption expenditure through its agency, Statistics Canada. With the current high inflation rate, and the possibility that our country is heading to a recession, good financial health has now become more important than ever.

What If Finance helps you assess your personal finance by comparing your annual expenses with the national average and gives you advice based on that.
